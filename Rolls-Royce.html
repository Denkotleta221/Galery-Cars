Droptail
A modern interpretation of the roadster body style, Droptail represents the next chapter of Rolls-Royce Coachbuild. Only four expressions of the motor car will ever be made, each the result of remarkable collaborations between Coachbuild artisans and ambitious, visionary clients. The first commission, Rolls-Royce La Rose Noire Droptail, embodies the allure of the Black Baccara rose.
